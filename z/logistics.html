There is no leadership within Zeteism other than the Archons who have develop the core teachings. The whole development of Zeteism is copyleft and is covered by the GFDL license, meaning that you can alter whatever you like and make it your own and even share it, as long as your version also adopts the GFDL license. Further, this implies a non dogmatic outlook on the whole organization. Zeteism is a non-profit organization. In the future we wish to provide some level of free legitimate psychological services (actual certified psychologists) for our members, excersice and meditation training, and most importantly, a positive community for individual development.
